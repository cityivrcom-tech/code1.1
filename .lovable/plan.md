

# GigOn - Investor-Facing Website Plan

## Overview
A professional, warm, and approachable single-page website designed to showcase GigOn's mission to democratize internet access for rural Bharat. The website will be optimized for investor presentations with a clean, community-focused aesthetic.

---

## Design Direction
- **Color Palette**: Warm oranges and earthy tones representing India/Bharat, combined with professional blues for trust and technology
- **Typography**: Clean, modern fonts with excellent readability
- **Imagery**: Visuals depicting rural India, community connections, and digital empowerment
- **Feel**: Approachable yet professional - conveying both social impact and investment potential

---

## Website Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Powerful headline: "Connecting Unconnected Bharat"
- Tagline about democratizing internet for a more inclusive digital future
- Eye-catching background with imagery representing rural connectivity
- Call-to-action button for investors to contact/learn more

### 2. Vision & Mission
- Clear display of the vision: "Connect 140 million people of Bharat"
- Mission statement about affordable, accessible, and reliable Wi-Fi 24x7
- Visual elements showing urban vs rural digital divide

### 3. The Problem
- "The Invisible Wall" - explaining the $200B digital divide
- Three key barriers: Price, Signal, and Broadband Poverty
- Statistics from TRAI about indoor signal issues (80% mobile data consumed indoors)
- Visual representation of the digital divide challenge

### 4. The Solution
- "Sachet Revolution for Data" - affordable micro-packs (₹2 to ₹100)
- PM-WANI compliant infrastructure explanation
- Key differentiators: Hyper-Local Mesh, Village-Cloud, Signal solution
- App preview showcasing the simple 1-2-3 user experience

### 5. Market Opportunity
- $1 Trillion Digital Economy by 2030
- Key statistics: 1.4B population, 1B mobile internet users, only 41M wired broadband
- World Bank multiplier showing GDP impact
- Visual TAM/SAM/SOM breakdown

### 6. Business Model
- Asset-Light, High-Margin infrastructure model
- The Flywheel: PDO partnerships, subscription revenue, platform fees
- Revenue streams: Subscriptions, Hyper-Local Advertising, B2B Data Insights
- 70% EBITDA margin highlight

### 7. Unit Economics
- Clear tables showing 4-month payback period
- Enterprise Device vs Low-End Device economics
- Low CAC, High LTV messaging
- Post-payback margin projections

### 8. Competitive Advantage
- Strategic Moats: Sachet Pricing, Signal Paradox, Last Mile Capex
- Comparison with competitors (Wiom, Telcos)
- PM-WANI regulatory advantage
- Why This Model Wins section

### 9. Go-To-Market Strategy
- "Kirana Trojan Horse" approach
- City-by-city rollout strategy
- Viral growth through referrals
- Community-powered deployment

### 10. Funding Ask
- $500K investment ask with 18-month runway
- Target: 200K users, ₹20M monthly revenue, ₹240M yearly
- Use of funds breakdown

### 11. Recognition & Partnerships
- Incubation: ATAL Incubation Centre, iSTART Rajasthan
- Industry presence: India Mobile Congress, TSDSI
- UN Sustainable Development Goals alignment
- Government support indicators

### 12. Team Section
- Founder: Shravan Pandey (14 years telecom experience)
- Operations: Debalina Roy (10 years telecom experience)  
- Mentor: Amitabh Singhal (Founder ISPAI, Ex-CEO NIXI)
- Emphasis on "For Bharat, By Bharat, To Bharat"

### 13. Contact / CTA Footer
- Contact information for Shravan Pandey
- Email and phone details
- Strong call-to-action for investor inquiries
- Social links (if any)

## User Experience Features
- **Smooth scrolling** navigation between sections
- **Animated counters** for impressive statistics
- **Subtle animations** on scroll for engaging storytelling
- **Fully responsive** design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- **Fast loading** optimized performance
- **Fixed navigation** header for easy access to all sections

---

## Technical Approach
Built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS for a modern, maintainable codebase that can be easily updated as GigOn grows.
